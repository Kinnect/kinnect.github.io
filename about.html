About

Hi! My name is Kevin. I am a detail-oriented business intelligence analyst and storyteller. I graduated with a BS in Finincial Economics from Binghamton University in Dec 2019. After graduation, I worked as a bursar coordinator at Relay GSE for 2 1/2 years, using analytics to understand student behavior, improve billing, and enable data-driven business decision-making.   • Machine learning models
